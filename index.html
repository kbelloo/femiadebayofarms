about

Femiadebanjofarms is an ultra modern farm at the heart of Ijebu, Ogun state. With several ponds across a strategically located corner of town, Femiadebanjofarms is one of the largest fish farms in the state and breeds tens of thousands of fish monthly to serve clients across multiple geopolitical zones in Nigeria

TEAM

CEO
FEmi Adebanjo
Femi Adebanjo is the founder & current CEO of Femiadebanjofarms. With a background in Electrical Engineering from the prestigious University of Ibadan and years of experience in Process Engineering & Operations Management with Procter & Gamble, Femi Adebanjo has successfully built the farm since inception to one of the most efficiently run and profitable farms in south west Nigeria & the country at large.
